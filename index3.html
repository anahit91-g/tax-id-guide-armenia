Q1: Are you a citizen of the Republic of Armenia?

Yes No

Do you have an Identification Card with Electronic Chip (eID) issued before February 15, 2023, and its PIN/PUK codes?

Yes No

Steps:

  1. Follow instructions at EKENG to activate and make the annual payment (3,000 AMD) for your electronic signature.
  2. Log in to the UNIFIED SYSTEM OF ELECTRONIC SERVICES FOR INDIVIDUALS tax portal, opt to sign in with "Yes em" system, and select "Log-in with ID card." You'll be assigned a Tax ID.

Do you have one of these?

  • Identification Card with Electronic Chip (eID) issued before February 15, 2023, but forgot PIN/PUK codes.
  • Identification Card without Electronic Chip.
Yes No

Steps:

  1. Visit any of the three mobile service providers (Team Telecom, Ucom, Viva MTS) and obtain Mobile ID with your Passport/ID and Social Security Number.
  2. Make the respective annual payment (3,000 AMD) to activate your electronic signature by following steps at EKENG.
  3. Log in to the UNIFIED SYSTEM OF ELECTRONIC SERVICES FOR INDIVIDUALS tax portal, opt to sign in with "Yes em" system, and select "Log-in with Mobile ID." You'll be assigned a Tax ID.
