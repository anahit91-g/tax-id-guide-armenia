Guide to Obtain Tax Identification Number in Armenia

Are you a citizen of the Republic of Armenia?

Yes No

Do you have an Identification Card with an Electronic Chip (eID) issued by February 15, 2023, and its PIN/PUK codes?

Yes No

Steps:

  1. Activate your electronic signature: EKENG (Annual payment: 3,000 AMD).
  2. Log in to the Unified System of Electronic Services and obtain your Tax ID automatically.

Do you have one of the following?

  • An Identification Card with an Electronic Chip (eID), but forgot PIN/PUK codes.
  • An Identification Card without an Electronic Chip.
Yes No

Steps:

  1. Obtain a Mobile ID from any mobile service provider (Team Telecom, Ucom, Viva MTS) using your Passport/ID and Social Security Number.
  2. Activate your Mobile ID electronic signature: EKENG (Annual payment: 3,000 AMD).
  3. Log in to the Unified System of Electronic Services and obtain your Tax ID automatically.

Steps:

  1. Book an appointment for an ID card: Migration and Citizenship Services.
  2. Obtain a Mobile ID from any mobile service provider (Team Telecom, Ucom, Viva MTS) using your Passport/ID and Social Security Number.
  3. Activate your Mobile ID electronic signature: EKENG (Annual payment: 3,000 AMD).
  4. Log in to the Unified System of Electronic Services and obtain your Tax ID automatically.
